'circe' - released today

 

georg and orri - along with hilmar örn hilmarsson and sigur rós touring guitarist kjartan holm - have created 72-minutes of brand new music to accompany this eye-popping film of a century of circus archive. the digital album is released today on sigur rós’s own krunk imprint. double vinyl and cd will follow on september 11 - you can order here.
