Hey R/GA, Marlon here with my front-end code sample. Here's a quick summary of what I did:

  • Included Twitter Bootstrap (with its responsive feature) and structured the page to adhere to its guidelines.
  • Added a static header and footer bar to give the page some uniformity. If this eventually evolves to a site with multiple pages, navigating becomes easy since the user does not have to scroll up and down to access the links.
  • Chose the Google Web Font, Average Sans, for readability and its subtle use of slants and curves in the characters to give the page some personality.
  • Now the interesting part: Made two simple AJAX calls to grab tweets from R/GA's Twitter account, as well as posts from R/GA's Facebook page. Each post and tweet is parsed via regex to look for mentions, hashtags, and links. Then each is displayed one at a time via a 5 second interval, along with the appropriate social-media call-to-actions.
  • Included Google Analytics to track page viewing.

Last but not least, thanks for taking the time to interview me, as well as viewing this page!
